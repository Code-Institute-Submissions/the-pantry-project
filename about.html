About Us

The Pantry is a small family run business established deep into the history of Nottingham. The family owned business, run by the Johnsens for 15 years, has been a staple of good food, quality entertainment and atmosphere.

A light vibrant interior that welcomes one and all as if they were part of the family themselves. The pantry offers a contemporary and modern approach to classic dishes to add an air of comfort and nostalgia in an up to date experience for all. With a function room available for hire and an 80 seater restaurant there is plenty of space to enjoy bistro style cooking in comfort.

Special dietary requirements are catered for and vegan and gluten-free options are always available.
