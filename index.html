About Us

The Pantry is a small family run business established deep into the history of Nottingham. The family owned business, run by the Johnsens for 15 years, has been a staple of good food, quality entertainment and atomsphere. A light vibrant interior that welcomes one and all as if they were part of the family themselves. The pantry offers a contemporary and modern approach to classic dishes to add an air of comfort and nostaliga in an up to date experience for all. With a function room available for hire and an 80 seater restaurant there is plenty of space to enjoy bistro style cooking in comfort.
